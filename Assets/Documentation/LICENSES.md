## Asset Search Notes

Kenney Space Shooter Redux and Kenney Space Shooter Extension were checked first because Kenney lists those packs as Creative Commons CC0. The official pages were visible in browser search, but the actual downloadable pack files were not reachable from this environment. OpenGameArt and other approved sources were considered next. Game-icons.net was selected as the reliable commercial-use source available during this pass.
